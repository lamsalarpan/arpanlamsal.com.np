About

I'm Arpan Lamsal, an aspiring IT student from Pokhara, Nepal, with a strong passion for web development, networking, and technology. From an early age, I've been fascinated by how digital systems work and how technology can solve real-world problems. That curiosity has driven me to constantly explore, experiment, and learn beyond the classroom—whether it's building responsive websites, understanding how the internet and computer networks operate, discovering new technologies, or volunteering at school events to gain practical experience.

Recently, I completed my +2 education and am preparing to begin my journey in Information Technology. Along the way, I've worked on personal projects, strengthened my programming skills, and explored networking concepts through hands-on learning and industry visits. Every project I build is an opportunity to improve my problem-solving, creativity, and attention to detail.

I enjoy turning ideas into functional projects and view every learning opportunity as a step toward becoming a skilled and well-rounded IT professional. I believe growth comes from continuous learning, collaboration, and taking on new challenges. My goal is to develop meaningful digital solutions, contribute to impactful projects, and keep expanding my knowledge as I grow in the tech industry.
